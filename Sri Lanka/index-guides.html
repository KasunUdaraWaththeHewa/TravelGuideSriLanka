Tour Guides with us

Sri Lanka offers a plethora of captivating destinations to explore. From the vibrant capital city of Colombo to the cultural hub of Kandy and the majestic Sigiriya rock fortress, there is something for everyone. Experience the cool climate of Nuwara Eliya in the hill country, wander through the historic streets of Galle, and soak up the scenic beauty of Ella. Discover the wildlife wonders of Yala National Park, unwind on the golden beaches of Bentota, and marvel at the ancient Dambulla Cave Temple. Don't miss the pristine beaches and underwater treasures of Trincomalee. Each place in Sri Lanka offers a unique blend of history, culture, natural beauty, and warm hospitality, making it a truly memorable destination.

Basil Rajapakse

Sigiriya, also known as the Lion Rock, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of Sri Lanka's most iconic landmarks. Located in the central Matale District, Sigiriya is a magnificent ancient rock fortress that attracts tourists from around the world.

Mahinda Rajapakse

Kandy is a picturesque city nestled in Sri Lanka's hill country. It is famous for the revered Temple of the Tooth Relic, which houses a sacred Buddhist relic. The city offers a blend of cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and a vibrant atmosphere. Visitors can explore the temple, enjoy the annual Esala Perahera procession, stroll along the scenic Kandy Lake, and visit nearby attractions like the Royal Botanical Gardens. Kandy is an ideal destination for immersing oneself in Sri Lanka's rich history and natural beauty.

Gotabaya Rajapkse

Jaffna, located in northern Sri Lanka, offers a fascinating glimpse into Tamil culture and heritage. Explore ancient temples, colonial architecture, and vibrant markets. Indulge in delicious local cuisine and relax on serene beaches. Jaffna provides a unique and off-the-beaten-path experience for curious travelers.

Namal Rajapakse

The Kandy Perahera is a magnificent cultural festival in Sri Lanka's city of Kandy. It features a grand procession with traditional dancers, drummers, elephants, and the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha. The vibrant atmosphere, stunning performances, and religious significance make it an unforgettable experience.

Ranil Wickramasinghe

Adam's Peak, or Sri Pada, is a sacred pilgrimage site in Sri Lanka's central highlands. Its conical shape and "sacred footprint" attract thousands of pilgrims who climb to the summit for spiritual fulfillment and breathtaking views.

Sajith Premadasa

Jungle Beach in Unawatuna, Sri Lanka, is a secluded and pristine cove surrounded by lush greenery. With its crystal-clear waters, soft sand, and tranquil ambiance, it offers a hidden paradise for beach lovers seeking a serene escape.

Anura Kumara Dissanayake

Haputale, nestled in Sri Lanka's central highlands, is a serene hill town with panoramic views of tea plantations and misty mountains. Explore the lush landscapes, visit the famous Lipton's Seat viewpoint, and indulge in the tranquility of this charming getaway.

Mr. Been

Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England," is a picturesque hill station in Sri Lanka. With its cool climate, tea plantations, and colonial charm, it offers a serene escape. Explore the scenic landscapes, visit tea estates, and enjoy the town's old-world ambiance.

Charlie Chaplin

Anuradhapura, located in Sri Lanka, stands as a testament to the glorious past of the island nation. As the ancient capital of Sri Lanka for more than a thousand years, Anuradhapura is a captivating destination that immerses visitors in its rich history, architectural marvels, and spiritual heritage. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a treasure trove of well-preserved archaeological wonders, boasting magnificent stupas, monastic complexes, royal palaces, and other structures that showcase the architectural prowess of ancient Sri Lanka.

The Dictator

The city showcases a fascinating blend of Buddhist and Hindu influences, evident in its temples, statues, and structures. Polonnaruwa is home to remarkable archaeological sites, including the iconic Gal Vihara, featuring colossal rock-cut Buddha statues, considered masterpieces of ancient Sri Lankan art. The majestic Royal Palace complex, with its intricate carvings and royal bathing pools, offers a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of the ancient kings.

Block and Dino

Katharagama, located in Sri Lanka's southeastern region, is a sacred pilgrimage town renowned for its religious significance and cultural diversity. It is a revered destination for devotees of multiple faiths, including Buddhists, Hindus, and Muslims, who flock to this vibrant town seeking blessings and spiritual solace.

Johny Sins

Mathale, located in Sri Lanka's Central Province, is a captivating district known for its natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage. This picturesque region is adorned with tea plantations, waterfalls, and the majestic Knuckles Mountain Range. It is home to notable attractions such as the Aluvihare Rock Cave Temple, where ancient Buddhist scriptures were written, and the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, offering breathtaking views. Mathale also boasts traditional spice gardens and vibrant festivals, making it a delightful destination for nature lovers, history enthusiasts, and cultural explorers.
